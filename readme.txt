Scroll Progress Bar displays a visual indicator of your location on a page. You may also activate smooth scroll.

== Description ==

Scroll Progress Bar will display a simple progress bar indicating the users location on a post or page. You also have the option to activate smooth scroll to enhance scrolling on your site.

You can alter the color of the progress bar, and select any or all registered post types on your site to display the scroll progress bar on.

Want to display the scroll progress bar on your single blog posts page, but no where else? No problem!
